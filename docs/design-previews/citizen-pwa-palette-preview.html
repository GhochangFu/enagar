UX note — why the canvas should be lighter

A full-page #FFCDAA peach reads playful and saturated — fine for marketing, but for a government service app it competes with CTAs and feels less calm. Professionals use 90% neutral + 10% brand colour:

  • Canvas → warm white (#FAF7F4) — barely tinted, paper-like
  • Peach / salmon → small accents (badges, 1px borders, optional card top strip) — not full bleed
  • Burnt + forest → buttons and KPI band — carry the identity
  • Cards → pure #FFFFFF on the warm white canvas for clear hierarchy
